Card Eligibility

Two Paths. One Card.

Every student can earn KENO and access the KUTL Card — but the path to eligibility reflects the commitment made on day one.

Tier 1 — Enrolled Student
Card on Day One

When you invest in your education with real enrollment, you get your KUTL Card immediately. No waiting. No milestones. Your card ships the moment your enrollment is confirmed.

  • ✦ Instant card issuance — activate on enrollment day
  • ✦ Real-time KENO spending — rewards spendable as they are earned
  • ✦ 250 KENO per course — up to $5,250 across 21 courses
  • ✦ Full staking access — earn yield on your KENO balance
  • ✦ Priority card tier upgrades — Guardian and Scholar tiers unlocked first
💳 Card Active Immediately Upon Enrollment
Tier 2 — Wealth Builder Scholarship
Card at Graduation

Scholarship students access the full 21-course curriculum at no upfront cost. KENO rewards are earned and locked throughout the program — unlocking in full the moment you graduate. Graduation means something here.

  • ✦ Full curriculum access — all 21 courses at zero upfront cost
  • ✦ KENO earned throughout — 250 KENO per course completed
  • ✦ Balance locked until graduation — protected and building the entire time
  • ✦ Card + full balance released at graduation — up to $5,250 unlocked at once
  • ✦ Graduation is a financial event — not just a certificate
🎓 Card + KENO Unlock Upon Graduation

Both paths lead to the same destination — a KUTL Card in your hand and KENO in your wallet. The difference is when you get there. Enrollment is the fast lane. Scholarship is the earned lane. Either way, Knowledge Is The New Capital.
