The $1.00 USDB Peg — How It Works

KENO is designed to trade near $1.00 USD. Right now liquidity in the pool is thin, so the price moves easily with small trades. As more liquidity providers add KENO + BNB to the pool, the price stabilizes and tracks $1.00 more reliably.

The UTL FeeCollector feeds 60% of all protocol fees to KENO stakers — this creates continuous buy pressure on KENO as the ecosystem grows. More volume → more fees → more demand for KENO → price rises toward peg.

How to Add Liquidity

Get KENO tokens

Buy KENO on PancakeSwap using BNB. Or purchase through the ICO page during the presale.

Go to PancakeSwap Add Liquidity

Click Add Liquidity below. PancakeSwap will open with the KENO/BNB pair pre-filled.

Enter equal USD amounts

Enter the amount of KENO you want to add. PancakeSwap will auto-calculate how much BNB to pair with it at the current ratio.

Confirm and receive LP tokens

Approve the transaction. You receive KENO-BNB LP tokens representing your share of the pool — redeemable anytime.
