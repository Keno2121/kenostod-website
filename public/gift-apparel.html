What's Inside the G.I.F.T. Apparel

Embedded Shield Technology
Every G.I.F.T. garment includes a smart shield panel with embedded LED blocks and a proximity sensor. The shield is the visual heart of the G.I.F.T. experience.

Proximity Sensor
Short-range BLE/NFC proximity detection identifies when two G.I.F.T. garments are within range of each other — triggering the progressive light sequence automatically.

Shield blocks illuminate one by one as distance closes — dim at 30 ft, brightening progressively until full illumination at greeting distance (within handshake range).

A unique harmonic chime sequence builds as Kenostodists approach — audible to both parties, escalating to the full Eureka sound at the moment of complete illumination.
