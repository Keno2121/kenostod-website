Section 2
Step-by-Step Flow of Funds
1
Member Enrollment — Fiat Payment to Kenostod
Member pays the Academy enrollment fee via credit/debit card, ACH, or other accepted payment method. This fiat payment goes directly to Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC's operating account. No crypto involved at this stage.
💵 Fiat (USD) → Kenostod Operating Account
↓
2
Course Completion — KENO Reward Issuance
Upon verified completion of each course, Kenostod issues 250 KENO tokens to the member's BSC wallet. Total potential: 5,250 KENO across all 21 courses. KENO is a utility token — not a currency or security. Issuance is a reward event, not a financial transaction.
🎓 250 KENO per course · 5,250 KENO total potential
↓
3
Member Requests KUTL Card Load
Member initiates a card load request through the Kenostod platform. Member specifies the USD amount they want loaded. Kenostod calculates the equivalent KENO at current market rate and deducts it from the member's platform balance. This triggers a redemption event.
📲 Member requests: e.g. $100 load = X KENO redeemed
↓
4
Kenostod Funds the Card Account via Cybrid API
Kenostod calls the Cybrid API to fund the member's card account in USD. Kenostod sends the corresponding USD from its treasury account to Cybrid. Cybrid credits the member's card account. KYC/AML has already been completed at onboarding. No fiat-to-crypto conversion occurs at Cybrid — Cybrid only receives and holds USD on behalf of the member.
🏦 Kenostod USD → Cybrid API → Member Card Account (USD)
↓
5
Member Spends at Merchant
Member uses the KUTL Visa/Mastercard (physical or virtual) at any merchant. Transaction is processed through the standard card network. Cybrid settles with the merchant's acquiring bank via Visa/Mastercard interchange. Member's card balance decreases by the transaction amount.
💳 Card Swipe → Visa/Mastercard Network → Merchant Settlement
↓
6
Interchange & Fee Settlement
Standard interchange fees flow back through the network. Cybrid retains its BaaS service fee as agreed in the partnership contract. Kenostod earns a share of interchange revenue. Remaining settlement completes to the merchant's bank. No KENO tokens are involved in this stage — all settlement is in USD.
📊 Interchange → Cybrid Fee + Kenostod Revenue Share

Section 4
Compliance & KYC/AML Notes
🔒 Regulatory & Compliance Position
  • All members complete KYC at enrollment — identity verified before any KENO is issued or card is activated.
  • KENO token is a utility/reward token, not a currency, security, or stablecoin. Issuance is a reward event for educational completion, not a financial transaction.
  • Kenostod holds USD in its operating account to back all card loads 1:1 — no fractional reserve, no leverage.
  • Cybrid only ever receives and disburses USD — no cryptocurrency touches the Cybrid infrastructure.
  • All card activity is subject to standard Visa/Mastercard AML monitoring and reporting requirements via Cybrid's existing compliance framework.
  • KENO redemption events are logged on-chain (BSC) and in Kenostod's platform database for audit trail.
