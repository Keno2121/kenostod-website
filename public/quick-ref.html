KENO Burn vs Buyback: Burn happens on card swipe (deflationary). Buyback = treasury uses interchange revenue to purchase KENO from PancakeSwap to refill the learn-to-earn reward pool. These are two completely separate mechanisms.

Education-Fi (E-Fi): Coined by Nickeo Coleman, 2026. First published March 14, 2026. USPTO trademark application in preparation. Urban Dictionary entry filed. Cite the date in all materials.

B.U.K. Banking Model: Neobank (BaaS partner, NOT a chartered bank). Phase 2 Q1 2027. Smart contract insurance requires licensed InsurTech partner model. B.U.K. is the tech + distribution layer only.

MetaMask Snap: PR #1459 open in snaps-registry. Once approved, snap works in regular MetaMask for all users. Flask is only needed for development testing. The snap is the E-Fi native wallet interface — transaction insights, tier progression, KUTL Card eligibility verification.

B.U.K. = Back Up Key Security: Security-first company. Dual chip: banking chip + key chip (replaces home and car keys). Real-time monitoring, auto police alert. Banking is the bonus, not the core. Separate from Academy — serves general public independently.
