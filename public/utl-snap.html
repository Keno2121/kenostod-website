Quick Setup (3 minutes)

MetaMask Flask requires snaps to run from your local computer for security. Follow these steps to get running:

1
Download the UTL Snap package

Click below to download all the files you need:

2
Create a folder and save the files

Create a folder called utl-snap on your computer. Inside it, create a dist subfolder. Save the downloaded files like this:

utl-snap/
  snap.manifest.json
  serve-snap.js
  dist/
    bundle.js
3
Open a terminal and run the server

Open Command Prompt (Windows) or Terminal (Mac), navigate to the folder, and run:

node serve-snap.js

You should see "UTL Snap Server Running!" — keep this terminal open.

4
Come back here and install the Snap

Once the local server is running, click the button below. MetaMask Flask will pop up asking you to approve the installation.

Why localhost? MetaMask Flask requires development snaps to run from your own computer (localhost) for security. This prevents malicious snaps from being loaded from unknown servers. Once the UTL Snap is published to npm, this local setup won't be needed anymore.

How UTL Works

1. Opt In — Activate UTL on your wallet. A 0.1% fee is captured from transactions on the UTL protocol.

2. Fees Pool — All captured fees across every UTL participant are pooled together in USDC.

3. Distribution — 60% of the fee pool is redistributed to UTL participants. The remaining 40% goes to the UTL treasury for protocol growth and reserves.

4. Withdraw — Claim your accumulated USDC to your wallet at any time, or cash out to USD via Mercury bank transfer.

Fee Distribution

Stakers (you) 60%
Treasury 40%
USD Cashout: Accumulated USDC can be converted to USD in your bank account. Claim USDC to your wallet, then transfer to an exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.) to convert to USD and withdraw to your bank. Alternatively, use any fiat off-ramp service that supports USDC on BSC.
