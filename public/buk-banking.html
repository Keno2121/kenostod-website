The Technology

One Card. Two Chips. Total Control.

The B.U.K. card is not just a bank card and it is not just a security key. It is both — permanently fused into one card you carry in your wallet.

💳
CHIP 1 — BANKING
Gold Banking Chip
Standard EMV banking chip — swipe, tap, or insert at any merchant worldwide. Linked to your B.U.K. account and KENO balance. Powered by Bridge.xyz settlement infrastructure.
  • Spend anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted
  • Tap to pay with NFC contactless
  • KENO auto-converts to USDB at point of sale
  • 0.1% UTL fee captured on every transaction
  • Insurance coverage on qualifying purchases
🔑
CHIP 2 — BACK UP KEY
Security Key Chip
Encrypted NFC/RFID security chip that pairs with your home locks, car, office, and any B.U.K.-compatible smart lock. Tap the card to any paired lock — it opens instantly. Your phone is your backup.
  • Replaces home keys, car fob, office badge
  • Pairs with B.U.K. smart locks (sold separately)
  • Remote lock/unlock from the B.U.K. app
  • Grant temporary access to anyone via app
  • Instant revoke — lost card is auto-disabled

How B.U.K. Makes Money

Six Revenue Streams.
All from One Card.

B.U.K. is not a charity — it's a business with layered revenue from hardware, subscriptions, banking, insurance, and data partnerships.

📦
Stream 1
Hardware Sales
B.U.K. card + smart lock kit sold directly. Each new customer is a one-time hardware sale before they even pay a subscription.
$99–$299per card + lock kit
🔄
Stream 2
Monthly Monitoring
Like ADT but smarter. Monthly subscription for real-time monitoring, police dispatch coordination, and camera storage. Recurring — every month, every customer.
$29–$99per property / month
💳
Stream 3
Banking Interchange
Every swipe of the B.U.K. banking chip earns interchange revenue from the merchant network. Passive income on every purchase made by every customer.
1–3%per card transaction
🏥
Stream 4
Insurance Premium Share
B.U.K. partners with insurers who pay a distribution margin for every policy issued to a B.U.K. customer. Customers with B.U.K. monitoring get lower premiums — B.U.K. keeps the spread.
15–25%margin on premiums
⚙️
Stream 5
UTL Fee Capture
All KENO-related banking flows through the UTL Protocol. B.U.K. is a node in the ecosystem — every transaction generates a 0.1% fee that splits back to the treasury and stakers.
0.1%every KENO transaction

Stream 6
Technology Licensing
The dual-chip (banking + security key) architecture is patent-pending. Other banks, security companies, and card manufacturers can license the B.U.K. chip design — royalties on every unit produced.
