How UTL Works

1
Opt In — Activate UTL on your wallet. A 0.1% fee is captured from transactions that flow through the UTL protocol.
2
Fees Pool — All captured fees across every UTL participant are pooled together in USDC.
3
Distribution — 60% of the fee pool is redistributed to UTL participants. The remaining 40% goes to the UTL treasury for protocol growth and reserves.
4
Withdraw — Claim your accumulated USDC to your wallet at any time, or cash out to USD via Mercury bank transfer.
USDC Model: All UTL fees are collected and distributed in USDC (USD Coin), a regulated dollar-backed stablecoin. This means your accumulated balance maintains a stable dollar value. You can withdraw to your wallet or cash out to USD via Mercury bank transfer at any time.
