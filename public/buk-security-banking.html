Core Product
What the B.U.K. Card Does
🔑
Smart Key Replacement
The blue security chip replaces your home door locks and car ignition. One tap with the B.U.K. card unlocks your front door and starts your vehicle — no separate key fob needed.
📡
Real-Time Property Monitoring
Live monitoring of your home, vehicle, and registered properties through the B.U.K. app. See door status, motion detection, and entry logs from anywhere in real time.
🚨
Auto Police Alert on Break-In
If unauthorized entry is detected, the B.U.K. system automatically contacts local authorities — no manual trigger required. The alert fires the moment breach is confirmed.
🏦
Full Banking Integration
The gold EMV chip is a fully functional debit/credit banking chip. Spend anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted, access KENO rewards, and load your KUTL balance — all on the same card.
📱
B.U.K. Security App
Central dashboard for all monitored properties. Lock/unlock doors remotely, review entry logs, receive breach notifications, and manage your banking — from one app.
🔐
Multi-Property Registration
Register multiple properties, vehicles, and entry points to a single B.U.K. card. One card secures your entire physical world — home, car, office, storage unit.
