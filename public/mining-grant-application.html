❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn? ▼

Grant amounts start at $250/month (5 courses) and scale to $2,000+/month for graduates (21 courses). Plus, your Platinum RVT NFT (graduate benefit) earns 2% perpetual royalties from platform revenue.

When do I get paid? ▼

Grants are disbursed monthly. Upon approval, your first payment is processed within 5-7 business days. Subsequent payments are automatic on the 1st of each month.

Do I need to do anything to earn? ▼

Continue completing courses and stay active on the platform. Grant amounts increase as you complete more courses. The more you learn, the more you earn.

Can I trade or sell the KENO tokens? ▼

Yes! KENO tokens are real BEP-20 tokens. Once you receive them, you own them fully and can hold, trade, or use them however you want. After the PancakeSwap listing (post-ICO), liquidity will be available.

What if I don't complete all 21 courses? ▼

That's fine! Your grant is based on courses completed, not completion of all 21. You can start earning with just 5 courses and increase your grant as you progress.

Is there a limit to applications? ▼

Grants are first-come, first-served based on application date and eligibility. We have a limited number of grants available each month, so apply early to secure your spot.
