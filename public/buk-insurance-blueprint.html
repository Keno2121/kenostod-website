Section 01 — Overview

What is B.U.K. Security Banking?

B.U.K. — Back Up Key — is the world's first banking product that combines full financial services with real-time home and vehicle security monitoring on a single card and app. One relationship replaces three.

💳
The Card
Dual Chip Technology
The B.U.K. card carries two separate chips on one physical card — a standard banking chip (EMV) for all financial transactions, and a proprietary B.U.K. Security Chip that connects to your home and vehicle systems. They operate on completely separate encrypted channels. Two chips. Two layers of protection. One card.
📱
The App
Real-Time Control Center
The B.U.K. app gives cardholders a live dashboard of all their properties — home and vehicle. They can lock or unlock doors remotely from anywhere in the world, view live camera feeds, grant temporary access to visitors, and receive instant breach alerts — all from the same app where they check their bank balance.
🚨
The Safety Net
Auto Police Alert System
The moment a sensor is triggered — forced entry, broken window, unauthorized vehicle movement — the system immediately sends an alert to the cardholder AND automatically contacts local law enforcement on their behalf. Response time is measured in seconds, not minutes. No middleman. No waiting on hold.
🔑
The Backup
Never Be Stranded Again
Keys locked in the car? Forgot to lock the house? Kid needs to get inside? The B.U.K. card and app solve every lockout scenario instantly — no locksmith, no spare key hidden under a mat, no waiting. One tap unlocks whatever you need. Remote access revokes automatically when time-limited access expires.
Section 02 — Technology

The Dual Chip Breakdown

Two completely independent chips on one card — each with its own encrypted channel, its own purpose, and its own security layer. If one is ever compromised, the other is unaffected.

Chip 01 — Banking
💎
Standard EMV Banking Chip
Functions identically to any standard banking card. Works at every ATM and payment terminal worldwide. Supports contactless NFC payments. Connects to full checking, savings, and credit accounts.
  • Checking & savings account access
  • Credit line integration
  • ATM withdrawals globally
  • Contactless NFC tap-to-pay
  • Standard chip & PIN transactions
  • Zero-knowledge of security chip activity
Chip 02 — B.U.K. Security
🔑
Proprietary B.U.K. Security Chip
Operates on an independent encrypted channel — completely isolated from the banking chip. Communicates directly with B.U.K.-compatible smart locks and vehicle receivers. The card becomes your master key to every lock you own.
  • Home smart lock remote access
  • Vehicle door lock/unlock
  • Real-time security sensor monitoring
  • Automatic police alert trigger
  • Time-limited guest access codes
  • Independent of banking chip at all times
