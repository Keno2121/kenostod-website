Section 01 — Overview

What is G.I.F.T.?

G.I.F.T. (Graduate Identity & Fellowship Technology) is a patent-pending smart apparel system that embeds NFC and Bluetooth technology directly into clothing. When two G.I.F.T.-enabled graduates come within proximity of each other, their apparel activates a real-world recognition experience — connecting their verified blockchain credentials instantly.

🎓
The Mission
Community in the Physical World
Blockchain credentials live on a screen. G.I.F.T. brings them into the real world. Every graduate of Kenostod Blockchain Academy wears verified technology — their apparel becomes proof of what they know and a signal to every other graduate in the room. No handshakes needed. The clothes do the talking.
📡
The Technology
NFC + BLE Embedded in Fabric
Each G.I.F.T. garment contains two embedded technologies: an NFC chip (NTAG424 DNA with dynamic encryption) for tap-to-verify credential checks, and a BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) beacon for proximity detection. When two G.I.F.T. wearers come within range, both receive a silent notification — a real-time signal that they're among their own community.
🔗
The Verification
Blockchain-Backed Credentials
Every G.I.F.T. chip is linked to the wearer's verified Kenostod graduation record on the blockchain. Tapping another person's G.I.F.T. garment with a phone instantly displays their verified course completions, KENO staking level, and graduate tier — all confirmed on-chain. No faking it. No resumes required.
🌐
The Market
A New Category Entirely
G.I.F.T. isn't fashion tech. It isn't corporate swag. It's the first wearable designed specifically for credential-based community building — sitting at the intersection of smart clothing ($21.48B by 2030), blockchain technology, and professional networking. No direct competitor exists in this space today.
Section 02 — The Experience

How the Recognition Flow Works

From the moment two graduates enter the same space to the moment they connect — G.I.F.T. makes it seamless, automatic, and verifiable.

01
🧥
Wearer Enters Space
Graduate A walks into any room, venue, or event wearing their G.I.F.T.-enabled garment. BLE beacon activates passively.
02
📶
Proximity Detected
Graduate B's garment detects Graduate A's BLE signal within 10–30 feet. Both garments recognize each other as verified G.I.F.T. items.
03
🔔
Silent Recognition
Both graduates receive a silent push notification: "A fellow graduate is nearby." No alert sounds. No personal data exposed — just an awareness signal.
04
📱
Tap to Verify
Either person can tap their phone to the other's garment NFC chip and instantly see their verified blockchain credentials, graduate tier, and KENO standing — all on-chain confirmed.

Section 08 — Moving Forward

Proposed Next Steps

Simple, fast, and low-commitment to start. We go from conversation to prototype in four steps.

01
Discovery Call (30 Minutes)
We walk through the full G.I.F.T. technical spec — NFC chip type, BLE integration requirements, garment types, and volume expectations. You share your manufacturing capabilities and any existing smart apparel experience. We align on which partnership model fits best.
02
Sample & Prototype Agreement
We agree on a small prototype run — 5 to 10 garments across 2 to 3 product types. This is low-cost and low-risk for both sides. We test NFC pairing, BLE detection range, and garment quality. Prototype cost is shared or offset against the first full production order.
03
First Production Run
Based on the prototype results, we confirm the first full production order — sized to the current graduate cohort. We finalize pricing, royalty structure, branding placement, and delivery timeline. G.I.F.T. goes live to the graduate community.
04
Long-Term Partnership Agreement
With the first run complete and the community response measured, we formalize the long-term partnership — locking in preferred partner status, volume commitments, royalty rates, and first right of refusal on all future G.I.F.T. product lines. We build together from here.
