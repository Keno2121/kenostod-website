2. Nature of Business Operations

Kenostod Blockchain Academy LLC operates an educational technology platform specializing in blockchain education, digital asset literacy, and decentralized finance (DeFi) training. The platform provides interactive, curriculum-based blockchain courses through which users earn the KENO Knowledge Utility Token (KUT) — a BEP-20 utility token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) mainnet.

The platform's core revenue model includes course enrollment fees, licensing agreements for the proprietary KENO ecosystem, Flash Arbitrage Loan (FAL™) access subscriptions, and the Universal Transaction Layer (UTL) protocol — a fee redistribution system integrated directly within digital wallets.
