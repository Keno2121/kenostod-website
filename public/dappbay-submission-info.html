Full Description
Kenostod Blockchain Academy is a comprehensive blockchain education platform built on BNB Chain. Students complete a 21-course curriculum covering wallets, transactions, mining, DeFi, and arbitrage trading. Each completed course rewards 250 KENO tokens (5,250 KENO total for graduates). The platform features a live blockchain simulator, merchant payment gateway, exchange trading simulator with real market data, and AI-powered student support. Graduates join the exclusive Graduate Club with access to Flash Arbitrage Loan training and perpetual royalty NFTs (RVTs). KENO is a verified BEP-20 token with an ongoing ICO.
