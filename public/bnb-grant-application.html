5. Problem Statement

What problem does your project solve?
The blockchain industry faces a critical user onboarding problem. Despite massive growth, adoption remains limited because:

1. HIGH BARRIER TO ENTRY: Most people find blockchain concepts confusing and intimidating, with no safe environment to practice

2. RISKY LEARNING: New users often lose money learning through trial and error on live networks

3. THEORETICAL EDUCATION: Existing courses are lecture-based with no hands-on practice, leading to poor retention

4. NO INCENTIVES: Traditional education offers no tangible rewards for completing courses

5. ECOSYSTEM FRAGMENTATION: New users struggle to understand how wallets, exchanges, DeFi, and tokens work together

This results in millions of potential users never entering the BNB Chain ecosystem, limiting growth and adoption. Kenostod solves this by providing a risk-free, incentivized learning environment that transforms curious beginners into confident blockchain users.
