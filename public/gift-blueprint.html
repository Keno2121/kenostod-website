🎯 What is G.I.F.T.?

G.I.F.T. (Graduate Identity & Fellowship Technology) is a patent-pending smart apparel system that allows Kenostod Academy graduates to recognize each other in real life through embedded NFC/BLE technology.

The "Eureka Moment" Vision

When two G.I.F.T.-enabled graduates come within proximity of each other, their apparel creates a unique recognition experience:

  • Subtle light flickering or LED activation
  • Unique "evolution chime" sound notification
  • Smartphone notification with graduate verification
  • Blockchain-verified credential exchange

⚡ Core Technology Components

NFC (Near Field Communication)

Range: 1-4 cm (tap required)
Power: Passive (no battery)
Use: Identity verification, blockchain credential check, profile exchange

BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy)

Range: 10-100 meters
Power: Small battery (CR2032)
Use: Proximity detection, "Eureka moment" trigger

Micro LED/Vibration Module

Trigger: BLE proximity event
Effect: Subtle light flicker or pulse
Use: Visual recognition cue

Companion Mobile App

Connection: BLE pairing
Features: Push notifications, profile view, blockchain verification
