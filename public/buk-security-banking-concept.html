Security Banking

"We don't just secure your finances — we secure your life."

Conceived: Sunday, March 2, 2025 at 10:43 AM

The Vision

A revolutionary banking system that allows clients to not just monitor their financial accounts, but also monitor their home and vehicle in real-time — receiving instant alerts for break-ins and automatically notifying authorities on the client's behalf.

💳 Revolutionary Dual Chip Technology

The B.U.K Security Banking card features two separate chips on a single card — a world first in banking technology.

💎

Chip 1: Banking Chip

Standard EMV banking chip for all financial transactions — checking, savings, credit, ATM access, and contactless payments. Works with any existing banking terminal worldwide.

🔑

Chip 2: B.U.K Chip (Back-Up Key)

Proprietary security chip that connects to your home and vehicle systems. Lock/unlock doors remotely, monitor security sensors, and trigger automatic authority alerts — your backup key when you need it most.

Why Dual Chips?

Separating the banking and security functions ensures that if one system is ever compromised, the other remains secure. The B.U.K chip operates on an independent encrypted channel, never sharing data with the banking chip. Two chips. Two layers of protection. One powerful card.

🔓 B.U.K Lock/Unlock System

Locked out? Your card IS your backup key. Never be stranded again.

🏠

Home Door Lock/Unlock

Locked out of your house? Tap your B.U.K card on the smart lock reader or use the app to unlock remotely. Works with compatible smart locks installed on your doors.

🚗

Vehicle Door Lock/Unlock

Keys locked in the car? Use the B.U.K app or tap your card on the vehicle's B.U.K receiver to unlock. No locksmith needed — your card is your rescue.

📱

Remote App Control

From anywhere in the world, use the B.U.K app to lock or unlock your home or vehicle. Grant temporary access to family, friends, or service providers.

🛡️

Secure Authentication

Multi-factor security: Card tap + PIN, Biometric + App, or Emergency override with customer service verification. Your security is never compromised.

Real-World Scenarios Solved

  • 🔑 Keys locked in car: Tap your B.U.K card or use app to unlock — no waiting for locksmith
  • 🏃 Left for work, forgot to lock: Lock your home remotely from the app
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Kid needs in the house: Unlock remotely for them, then re-lock when they're safe inside

B.U.K Security Banking unifies everything into one card, one app, one relationship.
