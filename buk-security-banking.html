Model
How B.U.K. Operates Within the Ecosystem
1
Foundation Treasury Management
B.U.K. holds and manages the T.D.I.R. Foundation's operating treasury — the USD and USDC reserves used to back KUTL Card loads, student reward payouts, and liquidity pool obligations across the ecosystem.
2
Member Capital Protection
Ecosystem participants who hold KENO, participate in UTLFarm, or maintain card balances have their capital protected under B.U.K.'s security framework — not exposed to the open market without their consent.
3
Liquidity Partner Escrow
Capital deployed by strategic liquidity partners into The Sovereign Economy is held and managed through B.U.K.'s secured infrastructure — providing partners with confidence that their capital is stewarded responsibly.
4
Security Revenue Generation
B.U.K. generates fee revenue from security banking services, treasury management, and account services — contributing to the nine revenue streams of The Sovereign Economy and reducing reliance on any single income source.
